## 🎯 The "Why" & Motivation
*   **Core Pain Point**: What is the single biggest frustration or friction point with existing options?
*   **Why Build a New App?**: Why build this from scratch instead of adding a feature or plugin to an existing app/service? What makes a separate app necessary?
*   **Target Experience**: What should the app feel like (e.g., speed, simplicity, distraction-free, offline-first reliability)?
